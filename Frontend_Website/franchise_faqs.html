Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries about our franchise program.

You need a minimum space of 500 sq. ft., qualified faculty for the courses you wish to offer, and a passion for promoting cultural education. Prior experience in education is a plus but not mandatory.

The fee structure varies based on the city tier and the number of courses you plan to offer. Please fill out the registration form to receive a detailed brochure with financial specifics.

We do not provide teachers directly, but we assist in the recruitment process by sharing profiles from our database. We also conduct mandatory training for your hired faculty.

Yes, you can run other non-conflicting courses. However, Edvayu certification will only be awarded for courses that follow our prescribed syllabus and examination process.

Typically, the process from application to agreement signing takes about 10-15 working days, provided all documents are in order.
