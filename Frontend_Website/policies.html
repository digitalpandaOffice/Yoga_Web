Privacy Policy

At Edvayu Educational Foundations, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or enroll in our programs.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you apply for admission, register for an exam, or sign up for our newsletter. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and educational background.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process your admissions and exam registrations.
  • To communicate with you about your academic progress and institute updates.
  • To improve our educational programs and website functionality.

Terms of Service

By accessing this website and enrolling in our courses, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service and all applicable laws and regulations.

Intellectual Property

All course materials, including syllabi, study guides, and video lectures, are the intellectual property of Edvayu Educational Foundations. Unauthorized distribution or reproduction is strictly prohibited.

Student Code of Conduct

We expect all students to maintain high standards of integrity and respect. This includes:

  • Respecting faculty, staff, and fellow students.
  • Adhering to academic honesty in all examinations and assignments.
  • Preserving the sanctity of our cultural and artistic traditions.

Refund Policy

Application fees are non-refundable. Tuition fees may be refunded partially if a withdrawal request is made within 15 days of the course start date, subject to administrative deductions.
