# Required PDF Field Names

Please open your PDF in a form editor (like LibreOffice Draw or Adobe Acrobat) and create Text Fields with the exact names listed below.

## Section 1: Centre Details
1. **CentreName**
2. **OwnerName**
3. **OwnerRelation** (for S/o, w/o, etc.)
4. **OwnerDOB**
5. **CentreAddress**
6. **OpeningDate**
7. **CentreArea**
8. **ToiletAvailable** (Expected values: "Yes" or "No")
9. **AuthorizationFor**

## Faculty Details (Table)
*Create 3 rows of fields for up to 3 faculty members*
10. **FacultyName_1**
11. **FacultyQual_1**
12. **FacultyExp_1**
13. **FacultyName_2**
14. **FacultyQual_2**
15. **FacultyExp_2**
16. **FacultyName_3**
17. **FacultyQual_3**
18. **FacultyExp_3**

## Payment Details
19. **AffiliationFee**
20. **PaymentDate**
21. **PaymentMode**
22. **TxnId**
23. **Remarks**

## Submission Details (Section 1)
24. **SubmissionDate**
25. **Place**

## Section 2: Centre Head Profile
26. **ProfileName**
27. **ProfileRelationName** (Father/Husband)
28. **ProfileDOB**
29. **Religion**
30. **Gender**
31. **Languages**
32. **PermanentAddress**
33. **Qualification**
34. **ProfileExperience**
35. **Email**
36. **ContactNumbers**
37. **Whatsapp**
38. **AboutFamily**

## Submission Details (Section 2)
39. **SubmissionDateProfile**
40. **PlaceProfile**
