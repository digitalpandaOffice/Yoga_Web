Onboarding Process

A simple, transparent journey to becoming an authorized Edvayu center.

01

Online Application

Fill out the franchise registration form on our website with your institution's details and infrastructure capabilities.

Apply Now
02

Initial Screening

Our team reviews your application to ensure it meets our eligibility criteria regarding space, faculty, and location.

03

Site Inspection & Discussion

A representative may visit your center (physically or virtually) to verify facilities. We discuss terms and clarify doubts.

04

Agreement & Fees

Sign the franchise agreement and pay the initial franchise fee to formalize the partnership.

05

Training & Setup

We provide the starter kit, curriculum access, and conduct training for your center head and faculty.

06

Grand Launch

You are now an authorized Edvayu center! We assist with the launch announcement and initial student enrollment drive.
